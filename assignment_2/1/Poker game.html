Poker Hand

Status :

One pair : Pair of Jacks or better.

Two pairs : Two pairs of equal cards.

Three of a kind : Three equal cards.

Straight : Five cards in sequence,
but not in the same suit.

Flush : Five cards of the same suit.

Full House : Three of a kind plus
one pair.

Four of a kind : Four equal cards.

Straight Flush : Five consecutive
cards in the same suit. A straight
flush with an ace as the highest
card is called a "Royal Flush".
